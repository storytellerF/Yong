Apps registering for non-system broadcasts should use the `ContextCompat#registerReceiver` APIs with flags set to either `RECEIVER_EXPORTED` or `RECEIVER_NOT_EXPORTED`. If you are not expecting broadcasts from other apps on the device, register your receiver with `RECEIVER_NOT_EXPORTED` to protect your receiver on all platform releases.
